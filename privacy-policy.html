Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or use Iezyverse Background Remover ("the Service").

Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device.

Additionally, as you browse the Service, we collect information about the individual web pages that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Service, and information about how you interact with the Service.

When you use our Background Removal tool:

  • We process the images you upload to remove backgrounds
  • These images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Original and processed images are automatically deleted from our temporary storage after processing is complete

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process and complete transactions, and send related information including confirmations
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Service
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, destruction, or alteration. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

No Data Storage

Iezyverse Background Remover is designed with privacy in mind. We do not permanently store your uploaded images. All image processing is done in real-time, and images are automatically removed from our temporary storage once processing is complete.

Analytics

We may use third-party Service Providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier. We use functional cookies that are necessary for the operation of our website. These cookies do not track your browsing activity on other websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By visiting the contact section on our website
