Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes! Iezyverse Background Remover is completely free to use. No hidden fees, no credit card required, and no registration needed.

How accurate is the background removal?

Our Python backend uses advanced AI algorithms to provide highly accurate results, even with complex images and fine details like hair.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. We process your images in real-time and do not store them on our servers. Your privacy is important to us.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and HEIC. You can download your processed images in PNG format with transparency.
